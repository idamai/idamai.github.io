For the love of Singing

Singing is one of Iggy's greatest passions. He started singing since kindergarten. Without any professional music education, he went on to compete in his first singing competition during his third primary school year. He joined Cathedral Basillica of Saints Peter and Paul Choir from January 2014 to December 2014. Currently he is back in Raffles Hall Voices of the National University of Singapore.

For the love of Musical

Iggy's first musical was a 2011 production done by National University of Singapore students, Ca Bau Kan. He was back on stage in 2012, in the musical Canting. He joined as a supporting cast member and dancer. He went to the States for a year and attended many musical shows to do a deeper research in formulas for musical productions. Currently he is taking "Exploration of Musical Production" course taught in NUS.

For the love of Building

Iggy is a developer too! One of his projects was LeapVis, which won the Best Data Visualization Award in PennAppsX. He is currently developing an enterprise grade system for his own startup. Check out his GitHub at https://github.com/idamai
